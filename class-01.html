Flujo de la Clase / Class Flow (90 minutes)

5 minutes

Canción de Entrada / Walk-on Song

Song: "Me Llamo Ana"

  • Simple physical greeting activity (handshakes, introductions in English)
  • Set the tone: This class is different and fun!
  • Students hear the song for the first time - they'll work with it in homework
10 minutes

Agenda + Course Overview

Key points to cover:

  • What makes this course different from traditional Spanish classes
  • The Ronda explained (demonstrate, don't just tell)
  • Participation rubric introduction
  • Grade breakdown overview
  • Building a learning community together
10 minutes

Expectations & Getting to Know You

Student Expectations Discussion:

  • Daily commitment: 5-7 minutes Leitner box practice (non-negotiable)
  • Participation: 30% of grade - show up, engage, take risks
  • Homework: Two 30-minute sessions per week (self-grading when possible)
  • Assessments: Performance-based (oral + written), not traditional tests
  • Growth mindset: Mistakes = learning. We celebrate trying!
Community Agreement: Respect, support each other, have fun, take risks. This is a safe space to learn!
7 minutes

Mini-Conferencia / Mini-Lecture: "The Leitner Box System"

Key concepts to discuss after video:

  • Why spaced repetition works (science-backed!)
  • The 4 boxes: Daily to Wednesday to Friday to Sunday (quiz words!)
  • Words move forward when correct, backward when wrong
  • Daily commitment: 5-7 minutes only
  • Goal: Get words to Sunday Box for quizzes
40 minutes

LA RONDA - Modified for Day 1

Students divided into 4 cohorts (5 students each) - Each spends 10 minutes at each actividad

Note: This is the first time, so explain as you go! Students will learn the routine.

Actividad COLORADO: PROFESSOR - MÉXICO (10 min)

  • Administer Day 1 questions:
  • What do you want to accomplish with Spanish?
  • How much time will you dedicate weekly?
  • What Spanish do you already know? (Timed 5-minute word list - write down every Spanish word you can think of!)
  • Get to know each student personally
  • Assign cohort: You are MÉXICO - you'll use Mexican accent for all assignments

Actividad VERDE: COHORT STRUCTURE - REPÚBLICA DOMINICANA (10 min)

  • Instructor assigns cohort: You are REPÚBLICA DOMINICANA - you'll use Dominican accent
  • FaceTime policy explained: Can FaceTime in up to 3 times without absence penalty (not on presentation days)
  • Choose homework buddies within your cohort
  • Establish homework circle order (rotation for assignments/helping each other)
  • Exchange contact information
  • Ice breaker: Two truths and a lie
Your cohort is your support system! You'll help each other through homework, practice together, and perform together.

Actividad CELESTE: IPA EXPLORATION - ARGENTINA (10 min)

Instructor assigns cohort: You are ARGENTINA - you'll use Argentinian accent

Featured: IPA Pronunciation App

  • Introduction: What is the International Phonetic Alphabet?
  • Practice with 5 simple Spanish sounds: /a/, /e/, /i/, /o/, /u/ using the app
  • Try typing Spanish words into the app and see the Argentinian pronunciation
  • Why pronunciation matters from day one

Actividad AZUL: FEAR & EXCITEMENT - ESPAÑA (10 min)

Instructor assigns cohort: You are ESPAÑA (Cádiz) - you'll use Spanish accent

  • Written reflection (can be anonymous if students prefer)
  • What are you most afraid of in this Spanish class?
  • What are you most looking forward to?
  • Any challenges or circumstances we should know about? (scheduling conflicts, disabilities, etc.)
  • This helps instructor customize support
