Actividad AZUL: VOCABULARY QUIZ GAME - ESPAÑA (7 min)

(3-min timer for each round)

Materials: Vocabulary Sheet #1, students' flashcards

How to Play:

  • Setup (30 sec): Sit in circle, first person is Caller
  • Round 1 (3 min): Caller shows flashcard, draws picture, or says English word
  • Contestants answer in Spanish going around circle
  • Correct answer: Caller writes ✓ next to student's name
  • Wrong answer: Write name (no check), move to next student
  • Round 2 (3 min): New Caller, continue with more words
  • Wrap-up (30 sec): Count checks, most wins! Turn in sheet
Tracking Example:
Word 1: sí → María ✓ | Juan | Pedro
Word 2: ser → Juan | Pedro ✓
