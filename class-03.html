Mini-Conferencia / Mini-Lecture: "Spanish Verb Patterns"

Understanding Spanish verbs - The R Rule

Rule #1: R = "to"

  • caminar = to walk
  • hablar = to speak
  • vivir = to live
  • The R in Spanish verbs it is NEVER pronounced like English R

Rule #2: Drop the R = él/ella/usted form

  • caminar → camina = he /she walks
  • hablar → habla = he /she speaks
  • vivir → vive = he /she lives
Question for You All: What do you all notice about the colored letters in our texts and in the song?

Tarea / Homework (Due before Class 4)

DAILY PRACTICE 5-7 min/day

Leitner Box Review (Every day!)

  • Every single day: Review cards in your Daily Box
  • Move cards forward when correct, backward when incorrect
  • Add the 20 NEW words from Week 2 to your Daily Box
  • Goal for Wednesday: Move words to Sunday Box for next quiz
💡 Week 2 Vocabulary (20 NEW words): vivir, gozar, reír, a veces, llega, bailar, limpiar, lluvia, toma, entender, solo, puedes, vencer, llorar, duele, olvida, escuchar, encontrar, gente, siempre

APARTMENT HUNT IN YOUR BARRIO 20 minutes

Find an apartment in your cohort's neighborhood

  • Use Google Maps to explore your assigned neighborhood
  • Choose an apartment/building on the suggested street (everyone in your cohort lives on the same street!)
  • Write the address in Spanish format
  • Describe where it is : "Está en la calle..."
  • List 3-4 nearby places: "Cerca de la tienda, la escuela..."
Submit: A short paragraph (4-5 sentences) describing your apartment's location

SONG CLOZE: "¿Dónde Está el Baño?" 15 minutes

Listen and Fill in the Blanks

  • Listen to "¿Dónde Está el Baño?" multiple times
  • Complete the cloze activity (fill-in-the-blank worksheet provided on course site)
  • Focus on: dónde, está, baño, and key vocabulary
  • Watch the colored letters! What do you all notice?
Observation: Write 2-3 sentences about what the colored letters represent in the song

NEW VOCABULARY CARDS + MNEMONICS 15 minutes

Create mnemonics for Week 2 vocabulary

  • Add the 20 NEW words to your Leitner box Daily section
  • Divide the work: Each cohort member creates mnemonics for 5 words (so 4 people = 20 words total)
  • Make your mnemonics creative, funny, or memorable!
  • Submit your 5 mnemonics via course site
  • Share with your cohort! The best mnemonics earn points on the first exam
💡 Remember: Good mnemonics connect the Spanish word to its English meaning in a memorable way!

TPR PRONOUN PHOTOS 10 minutes

Total Physical Response - Gesture Practice

  • Take a photo of yourself gesturing each pronoun:
  • Yo (I) - point to yourself
  • Tú (you, buddy) - point forward
  • Él/Ella (he/she) - point to the side
  • Usted (you, formal) - respectful gesture
  • Nosotros (we) - arms spread wide
  • Ustedes (you all) - point to group
  • Ellos/Ellas (they) - point to distant group
Submit: 7 photos showing each pronoun gesture via course site
⏱️ Total Homework Time:
  • Daily Leitner Box: 5-7 minutes per day
  • Apartment Hunt: 20 minutes
  • Song Cloze: 15 minutes
  • Vocabulary Cards + Mnemonics: 15 minutes
  • TPR Photos: 10 minutes
  • TOTAL: Approximately 60 minutes + daily 5-7 min practice
