Mini-Conferencia / Mini-Lecture: "Infinitives - The R Rule"

Understanding Spanish Infinitives

The Golden Rule: R = "to"

When you see an R at the end of a Spanish verb, it means "to" in English:

  • caminar = to walk
  • hablar = to speak
  • vivir = to live
  • comer = to eat
  • escuchar = to listen

From Infinitive to Conjugated Form

Remember from Class 3: Drop the R = √©l/ella/usted form:

  • caminar ‚Üí camina = he/she walks
  • hablar ‚Üí habla = he/she speaks
  • comer ‚Üí come = he/she eats
üéØ Key Takeaway: Every time you see an R at the end of a Spanish word, think "to" !
