Mini-Conferencia / Mini-Lecture: "El Futuro - The Future"

Talking About What We Are Going To Do

The Future Formula:

voy a / va a / vamos a + INFINITIVE

  • Yo voy a hablar = I'm going to speak
  • Tú vas a bailar = You're going to dance
  • Él/Ella va a vivir = He's/She's going to live
  • Nosotros vamos a comer = We're going to eat
  • Ellos van a ir = They're going to go

Examples from "Vivir Mi Vida":

  • Voy a reír = I'm going to laugh
  • Voy a bailar = I'm going to dance
  • Voy a gozar = I'm going to enjoy
  • Voy a vivir = I'm going to live
Key Pattern: Always use the INFINITIVE (the form with R) after voy a / va a / vamos a!
