Bienvenidos / Welcome!

This is not your typical Spanish class. Here, we focus on high-frequency vocabulary, perfect pronunciation through IPA (International Phonetic Alphabet), and spaced repetition techniques that ensure you retain what you learn.

By the end of this course, you will be able to converse at a Novice-High level, recall 250+ vocabulary words instantly, understand the nine verb conjugations, and confidently navigate your specialized topic in Spanish.

Get ready for a different kind of language learning experience!

Course Structure: The Three Acts

Act One: Building the Foundation

Weeks 1-5 | Classes 1-10
  • Establish daily routine & classroom structure
  • Master IPA for pronunciation
  • Learn mnemonics & Leitner box system
  • Build cohort trust & community
  • Acquire high-frequency vocabulary (70% fundamental)
  • TPR (Total Physical Response) with gestures

Act Two: The Grammar Tour

Weeks 6-9 | Classes 11-18
  • The Grammar Tour: spatial learning of tenses
  • Basement = Past | Main Floor = Present & Subjunctive | Upstairs = Future
  • Introduction to the 9 verb conjugations
  • Vocabulary shift to 50-50 (fundamental vs. specialized)
  • Integration of grammar into conversation
  • Midterm performance assessment

Act Three: Specialized Application

Weeks 10-14 | Classes 19-28
  • Focus on course-specific content (Costa Rica, Medical Spanish, etc.)
  • Vocabulary shift to 20-80 (80% specialized topic)
  • Real-world application of grammar & vocabulary
  • Cultural immersion & context
  • Final performance-based assessment
  • Speaking like a confident 24-month-old!
